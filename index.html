Vitaliy Krasovskyy - Comedian

Vitaliy Krasovskyy is a 23-year-old Ukrainian comedian making waves in the Netherlands with his sharp wit and dark humor. Growing up in Crimea and now navigating life in a foreign country, he brings a fresh perspective to the stage, mixing personal stories with social commentary. Vitaliy’s humor dives into everything from the strange world of modern convenience to his own existential crises, all delivered with an approachable, playful edge.
